Peshawar

1. Location & History
  • Capital of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK), near the Afghanistan border.
  • One of South Asia oldest cities, over 2,000 years old.
2. Culture & People
  • Predominantly Pashtun population; Pashto is widely spoken.
  • Famous for hospitality, bazaars, and street food.
3. Economy & Landmarks
  • Economy: Trade, agriculture, handicrafts, small industries.
  • Famous landmarks: Qissa Khwani Bazaar, Bala Hisar Fort, Mahabat Khan Mosque.
4. Cities near Peshawar:
  • Charsadda
  • Mardan
