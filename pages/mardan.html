Mardan

1. Location & History
  • City and district in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, east of Peshawar.
  • Important center of the Gandhara civilization (Takht-i-Bahi Buddhist ruins nearby).
2. Culture & People
  • Rich in traditions of hospitality, poetry, and sports (especially cricket and wrestling).
  • Known for strong cultural and family values.
3. Economy & Landmarks
  • Famous for Takht-i-Bahi ruins (UNESCO World Heritage Site) and Mardan sugar mills.
  • Known for sports goods and handicrafts.
4. Cities near Mardan:
  • Peshawar
  • Charsadda
