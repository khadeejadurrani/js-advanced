Charsadda

1. Location & History
  • District in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, near Peshawar.
  • Known as the ancient city of Pushkalavati, dating back to Gandhara civilization.
2. Culture & People
  • Majority Pashtun population; Pashto is the main language.
  • Agriculture plays a central role in daily life.
3. Economy & Landmarks
  • Economy: Farming (sugarcane, wheat, tobacco), trade, and handicrafts.
  • Famous for white sugarcane, tobacco, and traditional carpets.
4. Cities near Charsadda:
  • Peshawar
  • Mardan
