KHYBER PAKHTUNKHWA KPK

Location & Geography
  • Northwestern province of Pakistan, bordering Afghanistan.
  • Known for mountains, valleys, rivers (Swat, Kaghan, Chitral).
Culture & People
  • Major ethnic group: Pashtuns (Pashto-speaking).
  • Rich traditions in hospitality, music, and crafts.
Cities:
  • some of the cities of Kpk are:
  • Peshawar
  • Charsadda
  • Mardan
